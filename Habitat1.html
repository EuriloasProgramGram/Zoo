Ocean

The ocean is a place for creatures who lived in the water

like fishes and some mammals like seals.

Even plants that live in the ocean like kelp but most are endangered from human activities.

photo from worldatlas.com

Blue whales

Whales are mammals in the sea who breaths in the air but live in sea.

They are the largest mammals in the world but Whales are victims of endangerment due to human activities.

There's only between 10,000 and 25,000 left in sea.

Image from antarctica.gov.au

Sea turtles

Sea turtles are reptiles in the sea who are some herbivores or omnivores

Sea turtles are also endangered due to water pollution, habitat lost, climate change, and others.

There's only 6.5 million left in sea.
