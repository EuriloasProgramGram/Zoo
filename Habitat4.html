Burrowing owl

Burrowing owl also known as shoco are owls who like in burrows from their own or prairie dogs.

They are endangered like any animals from habitat loss, pesticides, and eradication programs

There's only about 2 million burrow owls remaining.

Image from hww.ca

Grasshopper sparrow

Grasshopper sparrow are small birds that hides in dense grass.

They also sing an unique song.

But they are endangered and is at risk due to habitat loss through fragmentation and degradation as well from construction.

There's only around 200 individuals which is horrible.

Image from abcbirds.org
